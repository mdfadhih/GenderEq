Mum-Friendly Workplaces

Maternal Benefits Offered By Companies

Paid Parental Leave

Companies that provide employee or government-funded paid parental leave for primary and secondary carers, with or without a qualifying time period, support work-life balance and gender equality by allowing parents to take time off to care for their newborns without facing financial hardship.

On-Site ChildCare

Companies that provide on-site childcare offer working parents the convenience of having childcare services close to their workplace, which can improve employee retention, reduce absenteeism, and increase productivity by alleviating the stress and time associated with arranging external childcare.

Parenting Workshops

Companies that provide parenting workshops targeting both mothers and fathers foster a supportive and inclusive work culture, promoting shared parental responsibilities and helping employees develop essential parenting skills.These workshops can improve employee well-being and better work-life balance.
